# The Growth Department — the TITAN Growth Constitution

> **Protected founder growth document.** This is the founder's constitution
> for TITAN's AI Growth Department — the standard the growth system is built
> toward and the doctrine for how each specialist comes alive. Like the
> [Founder Manifesto](../founder/FOUNDER-MANIFESTO.md) and
> [Experience Engineering](../experience/EXPERIENCE-ENGINEERING.md), it is
> authored by the founder; **engineering builds FROM it, never over it.**
> Part 1 is the founder's manifesto, preserved verbatim. Parts 2–4 are how it
> maps onto the architecture, where each specialist stands, and the platform
> reality it must win in — none of it is a build commitment until it appears
> in a milestone spec and (usually) an ADR.

## Part 2 — Architecture Translation

*How the manifesto maps onto what TITAN actually is. The manifesto sets the
department; this is the shape every specialist takes inside the codebase.*

### (a) The Shared Intelligence Law

**Every specialist reads from, and writes to, the ONE shared intelligence
layer. No specialist owns private data, ever.** The manifesto's insistence
that "every specialist learns from every other specialist" is not a slogan —
it is an architectural constraint. The shared layer is already the spine of
the product:

- **the Business record** — identity, trade (canonical taxonomy), coverage
  areas, lifecycle, contact, settings;
- **market intelligence** — CPL economics and demand signals per trade ×
  location ([ADR-025](../architecture/adr-025-market-intelligence.md));
- **measurement** — first-party site metrics, enquiries, response times
  ([ADR-030](../architecture/adr-030-lead-flow.md));
- **the activity / learning feed** — every artifact, decision, gate outcome,
  and note, appended and never overwritten
  ([ADR-023](../architecture/adr-023-business-spine-persistence.md)).

A specialist that needed a private store would break the law — the moment its
knowledge is invisible to the others, the "single intelligent organism"
fractures back into the siloed marketing the manifesto exists to kill. So
specialists never hold private data; they enrich the shared layer, and the
next specialist reads it there.

**The Growth Strategist AI is the Brain's growth-domain delegate**
([ADR-015](../architecture/adr-015-brain-orchestrator.md) pattern). Exactly as
the manifesto demands — "It never creates ads. It never writes content. It
never builds campaigns." — it **observes, decides, delegates, and learns, but
never executes a specialist's work.** It reads the shared layer, decides where
attention should go next, hands a scoped job to the right specialist, and
learns from the outcome the specialist writes back. It is the Chief Growth
Officer, not a doer.

### (b) The standard specialist module pattern

Every specialist — live, next-wave, or planned — is built to the same shape,
so they compose and stay legible:

1. **Inputs from shared intelligence.** The specialist reads only from the
   shared layer (Business record, market intelligence, measurement, learning
   feed). It never scrapes a private source or trusts a client-supplied
   figure the server can re-derive.
2. **Deterministic playbooks first, LLM reasoning second.** The default is a
   deterministic playbook — the reproducible, testable, explainable path
   (the ads build sheets of [ADR-031](../architecture/adr-031-ads-intelligence.md)
   are the reference implementation). LLM reasoning is layered on *top* of a
   deterministic core for the judgement a playbook cannot encode — never as
   the load-bearing floor.
3. **Artifacts through founder gates.** A specialist's output is an artifact
   that lands in **review** under a founder gate (the media gate of
   ADR-033/036, the build-item gate of ADR-031, the deal gate of ADR-026).
   Nothing a specialist produces reaches a customer, a platform, or a spend
   decision without the founder's approval.
4. **Outcomes written back to the learning feed.** What happened — approved,
   rejected, what it cost, how it performed — is appended to the shared
   activity/learning feed, so the Growth Strategist and every other
   specialist are smarter for the next decision. The loop closes; the organism
   learns.

### The Activation Law

**A specialist comes alive only when its data source and its execution
surface both exist.** No empty scaffolding — a specialist that cannot read
real inputs or act on a real surface is not "coming soon," it simply is not
built yet. Three consequences, stated plainly:

- **No empty scaffolding.** We do not ship a specialist shell that pretends
  to work. If the platform token or the data source is missing, the
  specialist waits.
- **Measurement before optimisation.** A channel is measured before it is
  optimised — you cannot improve what you cannot see (this is why Analytics
  and Tracking are primitive-live ahead of CRO).
- **Execution before automation.** A workflow is executed (and proven) before
  it is automated — you cannot automate a process that does not yet run (this
  is why Automation is planned, not first).

## Part 4 — Platform Reality

*Researched, July 2026. The manifesto's specialists must win in the world as
it actually is — not the world of five years ago.*

The advertising platforms have **automated execution itself.** Google AI
Max / Performance Max now own bidding and query matching; Meta's Andromeda
targets on **creative signals**, and manual audience targeting is deprecated
as a lever. A specialist that competes on "better bidding" or "better
audiences" is competing with the platform's own machine — and losing.

**So TITAN's specialists compete on the four levers the platforms cannot
own:**

1. **Creative volume & quality.** Meta's system rewards **10–15 conceptually
   distinct creative assets per campaign** — it needs variety to test into.
   This is exactly what **the media engine** ([ADR-033](../architecture/adr-033-media-pipeline.md)/[ADR-036](../architecture/adr-036-video-engine.md))
   produces at scale, under the founder gate. **The media engine is the Meta
   Specialist's core weapon** — the one input the platform's AI is starving
   for and cannot make for itself.
2. **First-party signal quality.** The platform AI is only as good as the
   conversion data it is fed. TITAN's **measurement → enhanced-conversions
   pipeline** ([ADR-030](../architecture/adr-030-lead-flow.md)) feeds the
   platforms cleaner, richer first-party signal than a typical competitor —
   and **revenue attribution multiplies this**, because optimising to revenue
   beats optimising to raw leads.
3. **Waste-policing.** Deterministic **negative-keyword and search-term
   hygiene** playbooks — **20–40% of a typical account's budget is waste**,
   and policing it is reproducible, testable, and immediately valuable.
4. **Landing experience.** **Generated premium area pages** ([ADR-028](../architecture/adr-028-multipage-area-schema.md)/[ADR-029](../architecture/adr-029-premium-primitive-set.md))
   — the destination the platform sends traffic to, which the platform does
   not build.

### GEO — the search shift

Search is moving to **AI answers**: a projected **~25% decline** in
traditional search, with **ChatGPT Search already ~18% of queries**. TITAN's
**schema / FAQ output is already GEO-correct** ([ADR-028](../architecture/adr-028-multipage-area-schema.md)) —
the structured, machine-readable, answer-shaped content the manifesto's GEO
Specialist calls for is what the renderer already emits.

The **AI-Visibility Monitor is the first new GEO activation**: weekly query
runs across **ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity** per business, **logged and
trended** in the shared learning feed. It has **no gatekeeper** (no platform
token to wait on) and **high sales value** (it answers the question every
owner will soon ask: "does the AI recommend me?"). It is the natural first
step out of "Foundation live."

### GBP — the local surface

**Five live Google Business Profile APIs** permit **review responses, business
posts, information sync, and Q&A at scale** — but **not** listing creation and
**not** review deletion. Those APIs require an **application for access**; the
**founder submits it alongside the Google Ads API token application** so the
GBP and Google Ads specialists activate together.

### The closing law

**Specialists never rebuild what the platforms give away for free.** The
platform will bid, match, and target better than any code we could write.
TITAN's specialists **feed** the platform machine (creative, first-party
signal), **police** its waste (negatives, search terms), **create** what it
cannot (film, premium pages, GEO-correct answers), and **coordinate** it all
through the Growth Strategist — one intelligent organism, exactly as the
manifesto demands.
